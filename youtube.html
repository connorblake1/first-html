Connor's Youtube Page

There are many frivolous things on Youtube, but I have found a few really credible and interesting channels that I am always learning from. Here are a few of my favorites. Click the logos to navigate to the main youtube page for each.

Veritasium

Made by a physics educator originally from Australia, Veritasium makes videos on anything current or historical about scientific advancements. One of my favorites is this video about how different parts of mitosis work with really great animations and commentary.

CGP Grey

CGP Grey creates animated videos on a variety of topic, all extremely dense with information and about interesting topics. One of my favorites is his video about federal land.

Numberphile

Numberphile is a channel all about interesting math. The creator invites mathemeticians from all over the world to host videos on interesting topics. One of my favorite videos is this video about the mandelbrot set.

Smarter Every Day

Smarter Every Day is a rocket scientist from Alabama that messes around with high speed cameras, large guns, and amazing science setups like schlieren. One of my favorite videos is this video about the shockwaves around bullets seen through schlieren imaging.
