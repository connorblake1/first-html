Connor's Quiz Bowl Page

My favorite extracurricular is by far quiz bowl. In a game of quiz bowl, 4 players from each team compete to answer questions on topics ranging from Russian literature to molecular biology. A lot of the topics are covered in school, but to be really successful, each player has to specialize in a subject not covered in school. Here is more about studying for quiz bowl.
